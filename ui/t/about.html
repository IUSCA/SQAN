RADY Quality Control Portal



Welcome to the RADY-QC portal!

You can review the status of automated protocol QC(QC1), and perform image QC(QC2) on this portal.

In the future, this portal is expected to allow execution of pipelines on datasets that have be approved for protocol and imaging QC.

You will need to sign in, and be authorized by an administrator to access data and perform various actions on this portal.
